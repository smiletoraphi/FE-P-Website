## Value for the Customer
Pewatron is a company of the Angst+Pfister Group and develops solutions in the areas of sensor technology, power supply and drive technology.

This says Thomas Röttinger, CEO der Pewatron AG:

> **_FE-P_** helped us locate an additional production partner in the South Asian region and carry out an assessment.


## Our Service
We have identified a suitable production partner, whereby in addition to technical requirements such as quality assurance, own development department and environmental protection, we also considered hidden requirements such as reaction time, flexibility and the company turnover / project size ratio.
